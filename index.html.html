About Yimali

Yimali Gonzalez is a High Impact Florida English high school teacher with an educational background in the fields of Psychology and English Literature and thirteen years of classroom experience. She has taught at the middle school, high school, and college levels. For the past nine years, she has been in charge of 9th grade English at AUC, leading classes and creating the curriculum. During the past year, Yimali worked with 10th grade as well. Miss Yimali Gonzalez has a true calling and passion for her profession, and this calling is reflected in her performance and long success. Also, as the well-rounded scholar with an interdisciplinary approach to education that she is, Yimali is at present conducting research at FIU's Department of Computing and Information Services in the National Science Foundation (NSF)'s Research Experience for Teachers 2018 summer program.
